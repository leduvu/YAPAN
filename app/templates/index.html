About

YAPAN is a Grammar Visualization Tool for Japanese, which covers around 620 JLPT grammar rules. It was developed as a part of a master's thesis. The code, the rules covered and more information can be found on github.

Japanese Grammar Visualisation

Paste the Japanese text you want to read into the text field, YAPAN will highlight grammar structures in the given text.

Hover over the highlighted words to see explanations and grammar construction rules.
